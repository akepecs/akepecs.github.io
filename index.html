Creating a New Claim

Validate OHIP Cards

Validates a patient's OHIP card and version number to ensure you'll get paid. If a card is valid the patient's information will be automatically filled in on the claim.

Save Drafts

Drafts are saved in the cloud for secure storage. You can finish claims at your convenience or just start drafts that can be filled out following a patient visit. Come back to claims when you're ready.

Recent Patients

Choose from your most recent patients to quickly submit claims during rounds or for other recurring patients.

Duplicating a Claim

Duplicated Claims

Previously submitted claims can be duplicated easily from the records screen. Just find the record and swipe left.

Whether you want to repeat a patient entire claim or simply copy the claim and fill in a new patient's information. The duplicated claim will contain today's date for admission and service date.

Filter

Previously submitted claims can be filtered by date using the funnel icon. Claims can also be organized by the claim's status using the "All/Rejected/Draft" selector.

Copying Billing Codes

Frequent Billing Combinations

Common sequences of billing codes can be inserted into a claim with two clicks. The billing codes are copied from a previous claim and include the claim's premium codes and units.

Add, Remove or Change Units

Of course, if the copied sequence of billing codes need to have a code added or removed that can be done right from the same screen. The unit of each billing code can also be modified right on the screen.
